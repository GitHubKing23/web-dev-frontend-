AI Quickstart for Small Businesses: A 7‑Day, Step‑by‑Step Playbook

Version August 12, 2025 • Built to capture leads and deploy your first AI win in one week.
Audience: Small Businesses Goal: Get Leads for AI Tools Format: Step-by-Step

Promise & Outcome

In 7 days, you’ll launch a simple, lead‑generating AI workflow that answers FAQs, captures emails, and books appointments — without hiring a developer. You’ll also track results with Google Analytics and know exactly what to improve next.

What you’ll build

  • Lead capture page + free guide delivery
  • Facebook Group invite on the thank‑you page
  • Lightweight AI assistant for FAQs/qualification
  • Automation from form → email → CRM/Sheet
  • Analytics + a simple ROI tracker

What you need

  • Google Account (Forms, Drive, Sheets)
  • Optional: Mailchimp/ConvertKit (free tier)
  • Optional: Zapier/Make (free tier)

Day 0 — Pick Your Highest‑Value Use Case

Choose one to start. Focus beats complexity.

Roofing / Home Services

  • 24/7 quote intake (address, roof type, photos)
  • FAQ bot: warranties, timelines, financing
  • Auto‑reply with next‑step + booking link

Car Sales

  • Lead pre‑qual (budget, down payment, must‑haves)
  • Trade‑in intake with photos & VIN
  • Appointment push to calendar

Junk Removal / Lawn Care

  • Photo + address intake → estimate range
  • Route batching suggestions
  • SMS reminders to reduce no‑shows
Pick one: What would save you 3+ hours/week or add \$500+/month?

Day 2 — Build the Lead Capture + Free Guide Delivery

  1. Create your guide in Google Docs → File → Download → PDF. Upload to Drive → set link to “Anyone with the link”.
  2. Create a Google Form with required Email + First Name.
  3. In Settings → Presentation → Confirmation message, add: “Thanks! Download your guide here:” + your Drive link.
  4. Embed the form on your site or landing page:
<iframe src="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/YOUR_FORM_ID/viewform?embedded=true" width="100%" height="900"></iframe>

Prefer email delivery? Remove the link from the confirmation, and send it automatically via Mailchimp/ConvertKit.

Day 3 — Thank‑You Page that Joins Your Facebook Group

The moment someone submits the form, send them to a simple thank‑you page with a strong community CTA.

Ask for the same email as a join question in your group to match contacts.

Day 4 — Spin Up a Lightweight AI Assistant

Create a simple assistant that answers FAQs and qualifies leads. You can start with a no‑code builder or embed a chat widget that uses your prompts.

Starter prompt (copy/paste)

You are a friendly assistant for {Your Business}. Your goals:
1) Answer FAQs concisely.
2) Qualify leads: budget, timeline, location, photos if relevant.
3) Push to book: "Would you like me to book a free estimate?" 
If the user shares phone/email, acknowledge and confirm consent.

Widget handoff lines

  • “To speed things up, could you share your budget range and timeline?”
  • “Want a human to call you? Drop your phone number and best time.”

Day 5 — Automations (Form → Sheet → Email → Alert)

  • Google Forms → Google Sheets (built‑in).
  • Zapier/Make: New row → send welcome email → add to Mailchimp → post a Slack/Discord alert → append to simple CRM Sheet.
  • Optional: Auto‑create a calendar event when someone books.

Sample welcome email

Subject: Your free guide + next steps

Hi {FirstName},

Here’s your guide: {Link}
If you’d like, reply with your budget and timeline and we’ll tailor a plan.

— {Your Name}

Day 6 — Analytics & Event Tracking

  • Add your GA4 snippet to your site’s <head> (once).
  • Fire a conversion event when the form is submitted or when the thank‑you page loads.

GA4 event example (thank‑you page)

<script>
window.dataLayer = window.dataLayer || [];
function gtag(){dataLayer.push(arguments);}
gtag('event', 'lead_capture', { method: 'google_form' });
</script>

Use UTM links on ads/posts to see which sources convert best.

Day 7 — Launch & Iterate

  • Post to your Facebook Page, Group, Instagram bio, and LinkedIn.
  • Ask 5 existing customers for feedback on the guide and chat assistant.
  • Every Friday: improve one thing (faster response, better email, clearer CTA).

Industry Playbooks (Swipe & Deploy)

Roofing

  • Form fields: address, roof type, square footage, photos
  • Bot: “Is this an insurance claim? What’s your timeline?”
  • Email: 3‑option estimate range + booking link

Junk Removal

  • Form: photos, item types, stairs? parking?
  • Bot: weight/volume estimate prompt; cross‑sell same‑day pickup
  • Automation: route sheet + SMS ETA

Car Sales

  • Form: budget, down payment, credit range, must‑haves
  • Bot: trade‑in intake (photos, VIN)
  • Automation: test‑drive booking & reminders
