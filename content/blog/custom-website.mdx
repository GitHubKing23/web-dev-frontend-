### 🚫 The Cookie-Cutter Trap

Many small businesses start with DIY website builders. While fast and cheap, these platforms come with limitations:
- 🐌 Slow loading times
- 📦 Generic templates that don&apos;t stand out
- 🔍 Limited SEO flexibility
- 🧱 Hard-to-scale architecture

Eventually, you outgrow the template and hit walls when trying to integrate new features.

---

### ✅ Custom = Control + Credibility

A custom-built website ensures:
- ⚡ **Performance** tailored to your users&apos; devices
- 🎨 **Brand consistency** across all visual elements
- 📈 **SEO-optimized** structure for better Google ranking
- 🔌 **Flexible integrations** with CRMs, APIs, or eCommerce
